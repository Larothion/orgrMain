Enjoy hunting with us

Relax and enjoy the Shiftshoot's only natural outdoor hunting facility. We maintain 850 acres of hills, flatlands, woods, open spaces, marshes, ponds, and streams.

Hosting hunting events

Whether you are an experienced hunter or new to the outdoors, Shiftshoot has something for you. We can organize an event for you and your friends, your family or a corporate event.

Why travel for hours when you can hunt close to home? Shiftshoot is located in Garden Prairie, IL, approximately 45 minutes west of Chicago. Our upland hunting areas contain native prairie grasses, sorghum & corn plots. We are also #1 shooting club in organizing theme parties and all kinds of events - we have all necessary facilities for it.

Our Clubhouse offers meeting space for occasions when business and pleasure mix, as well as shower and locker facilities for your convenience. The pro shop carries a complete line of hunting gear, including environmentally friendly shells and embroidered apparel. We can even completely outfit you and your party -- including firearms -- on a rental basis.

Our hunting club has Bar & Grill, which is open for breakfast and lunch six days a week. A full menu including burgers & sandwiches, pastas, salad, soup and more is available in a comfortable family-friendly atmosphere. After hunting, relax with friends and enjoy our fully stocked bar.

But the most attractive, of course, is our hunting facility. Thousands of acres of pristine natural wilderness make it one of the best hunting grounds in the United States with some of the best whitetail deer hunting in the state.
